Chapter -2

Without Main Verb Sentences

1.Is/Am /Are - है

Rule-[ s + is / am / are + ob + ow]

Examples-

वह इस समय कमरे में है

He is in the room at this time

वह मेरा दोस्त नहीं है

He is not my friend

आज बहुत गर्मी है

It is very hot today

क्या तुम उसके भाई हो

Are you his brother?

Practice Exercise

2. Was/ were - था

Rule- [s + was/were + ob + ow]

Examples-

वह मेरा मित्र था।

He was my Friend.

उस दिन वह मेरे साथ नहीं था।

He was not with me that day.

वे कल स्कूल में थे।

They were in the school yesterday.

क्या वह आपका दोस्त था?

Was He your Friend?

Practice Exercise

3. Will be /Shall be - होगा / होगा

Rule- [s + will be /shall be +ob+ow]

Examples-

वह मेरा दोस्त होगा।

He will be my Friend.

आपके साथ सब कुछ अच्छा होगा।

Everything will be good with you.

वह इस समय बिलासपुर में होंगे।

He will be in Bilaspur at this time.

क्या आपका दोस्त घर पर नहीं होगा?

Will your friend not be at home?

Mixed Examples:-

I like you a lot. - मैं तुम्हे बहुत चाहता हूँ।

I never gave him money.

There were many people shouting on the road. Will he have gone to school? I often go to his place/home. He sometimes comes my home. We wake up till late. With whose brother was I dancing? You’ve been searching something since afternoon. I know it will happen. You had gone there. I have seen Ram. His father came to my home. He has told me. Did he say? He never went. Suresh will meet me. We left for school at 7. Children never went. I don’t like this. Ram loves him/her. Everything changes with time. Money changes one’s character.
